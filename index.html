Welcome to Intelligent Automation Systems Pvt. Ltd.

We are the Automation Solution providers for wide range of industries like resins, paints, polymers, lubricants, drugs, food additives, Agrochemicals, Specialty chemicals, Water and Gas Pipelines.The company is registered with most of the consultants and executed direct orders for turnkey automation for GAIL, ONGC, HPCL, IOCL and numerous other companies.

We have a vast experience in executing Projects for Industrial Automation, Instrumentation & Electrical systems in all the major process plants like Power Generation, Refineries & Petro-Chemicals, Sugar, Steel, Fertilizers, Chemicals & many more industries throughout India & abroad too.

Our aim is to be an internationally respected Turnkey Solutions provider in Electrical, Instrumentation & Automation services offering efficient & economical solutions in various Industries by Serving our customers by adding superior value with our industry expertise, innovative systems and commitment to them.
